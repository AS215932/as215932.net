policy

Open peering. AS215932 is happy to peer with anyone meeting the requirements below, at any IXP where we are present or via private interconnection if that is more convenient.

requirements

  • Valid, maintained entry in PeeringDB
  • IRR objects registered (any recognized IRR)
  • RPKI ROAs published for all announced prefixes
  • Reachable 24/7 NOC contact
  • No default route, no transit leak, no bogons
